Personal care for your pet

Do you want the lovely company of a pet?

You can have all the benefits of having a pet to love, without the pressure of taking long walks or the financial burdens of being a pet owner.


We come to you!

Our curiers will pick up the pet at it's home location and then drive it safely to the warm hands of their new day-care-person. And at the end of the day, we make sure they get home to their home owners again.


There is plenty of love in the world, all we need to do is share!

Whether you are a pet owner or a person looking for some soothing company, we will bring you joy. Join us today and we will help you on your first steps to finding your match.

I am Edna, I am 86 years old and I live in Gamla Stan in central Stockholm. I absolutely adore animals and I would have my own if it were not for my bad knee. But I do so long to feel the warmth of soft fur beneath my fingertips. I will take good care of your pet for you, and we can watch "Days Of Our Lives" in my armchair together!
